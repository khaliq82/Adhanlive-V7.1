The 24-Hour Clock is Never Empty
Every dot is a mosque. Every ring is a prayer. Together, they reveal something extraordinary: Not a single hour of the day passes in the world without the Adhan. The global community of worship never sleeps.

280,621 mosques across every timezone keep the call to prayer alive around the clock. When Fajr begins in Southeast Asia, Isha is still echoing in West Africa. One unbroken wave, circling the globe.

Drag the centre dial to separate the five prayers into individual rings and see each one tell its own story.
