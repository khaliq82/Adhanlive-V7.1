Arc Explorer
Each of the five daily prayers traces a unique arc across the Earth as it travels from east to west, its shape determined entirely by the position of the sun at that precise moment. No two prayers draw the same line, and as the seasons shift, every arc shifts with them. Watching this change over a year is unlike anything else.
